Mickey Mouse History :
 
    Mickey Mouse is a cartoon character created in 1928 by 
    Walt Disney, and is the mascot of The Walt Disney Company. An anthropomorphic 
    mouse who typically wears red shorts, large yellow shoes, and white gloves,
    Mickey is one of the world's most recognizable fictional characters.

    /Created by: Walt Disney; Ub Iwerks
    /Designed by: Walt Disney
    /First appearance: Steamboat Willie (1928)
